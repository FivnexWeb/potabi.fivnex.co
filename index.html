 About The Potabi Foundation 

The Potabi Foundation is a not-for-profit organization that works on improvements in software, media, and education using technology. Our work is dedicated to the licensing and technoological and personal support. Meaning our work provides to the people.

Currently The Potabi Foundation does the vast majority of its work in it's software projects, as our size is still rather small. As we grow, both in donations and with volunteers and contributors, our goals are to start next on software licensing - as few licenses both protect the developer and the user, and those that do have other major flaws.

When the foundation is ready to support media - later education - then the foundation will work on better tools to distribute, use, and fund Creative Commons works. We want to work with other foundations to make a website where people upload and share Creative Commons licensed games, music, videos less than 120-minutes in length, and more.

And later, The Potabi Foundation will take a work-experience education system. We want to build education systems that allow people to learn about technology and get real work experience that will show that the person not only has several certificates but work experience using these technologies whether in more commercial-esque or experimental systems.
