The Four Core Philosophies

How is Potabi right for you?

Permissive Open Source

Allows you to use our software for your needs without having to abide by strict rules.

User-First Design

We take the time to listen to "PEBKAC" issues, and work to prevent them for new users.

Custom UI/UX

We work on our own custom UI/UX, which is a fork of the Lumina desktop environment.

Future-Focused

We focus on what is better for the future, not a strict adhesion to the past.
